🗑️ MeetFlow - Account & Data Deletion

App: MeetFlow - Meeting & Client Management
Last Updated: October 25, 2025

📱 How to Delete Your Account & Data

You can delete your MeetFlow account and all associated data directly from within the app. Follow these simple steps:

Step 1: Open the MeetFlow app on your device
Step 2: Tap the Profile/Settings icon (usually in the bottom navigation or menu)
Step 3: Navigate to Settings
Step 4: Scroll down to the Account section
Step 5: Tap on "Delete Account"
Step 6: Confirm your decision when prompted
Step 7: Your account and all data will be permanently deleted
⚠️ Warning: Account deletion is permanent and cannot be undone. All your data will be permanently removed from our servers.

📊 What Data Will Be Deleted?

When you delete your account, the following data will be permanently removed:

  • ✅ Your account information (email, name, phone number)
  • ✅ All meeting records and details
  • ✅ All client information and contacts
  • ✅ Meeting notes and attachments
  • ✅ Calendar events and reminders
  • ✅ Profile pictures and uploaded photos
  • ✅ App preferences and settings
  • ✅ All cloud-synced data from Firebase
  • ✅ All locally stored data on your device

⏱️ Data Retention Period

Immediate deletion: Your account and all associated personal data are deleted immediately upon confirmation.

Backup retention: For technical reasons, some data may remain in encrypted backups for up to 30 days before being permanently purged.

Analytics data: Anonymous, aggregated analytics data (crash logs, diagnostics) may be retained for up to 90 days for app improvement purposes. This data cannot be linked back to your identity.

🔒 Data Privacy

We take your privacy seriously. Your data is:

  • 🔐 Encrypted in transit using HTTPS/TLS
  • 🔐 Stored securely in Firebase Cloud services
  • 🔐 Only accessible by you while you have an active account
  • 🗑️ Permanently deleted when you delete your account

❓ Need Help?

Can't access the app? If you're unable to delete your account through the app, you can contact us directly:

📧 Email: support@meetflow.app
📝 Subject: Account Deletion Request
📄 Include: Your registered email address and reason for deletion

We will process your request within 48 hours and send you a confirmation email once completed.

📋 Additional Information

Partial data deletion: MeetFlow does not support partial data deletion. When you delete your account, all data is removed. If you only want to delete specific meetings or clients, you can do this individually within the app without deleting your entire account.

Account recovery: Once deleted, accounts cannot be recovered. If you wish to use MeetFlow again, you will need to create a new account.
